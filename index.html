TIC-TAC-TOE (C++/Arduino)

  • A game of Tic-Tac-Toe in which the player uses a standard IR control to make the plays against an AI opponent. It uses a 128x128p LCD display to show the board and another 16x2 LCD display to communicate with the player (ask for input, show the score...).

  • To resemble the experience of playing with a videogame controller, I mapped the 'forward,' 'rewind,' 'volume+,' and 'volume-' keys to behave like directional buttons, with the 'play/pause' button being used to confirm the play.
